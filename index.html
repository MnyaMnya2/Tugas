Princess Connect! Re:Dive (Japanese: プリンセスコネクトRe:Dive, Hepburn: Purinsesu Konekuto! Ridaibu) is a Japanese role-playing video game developed by Cygames. It was released in Japan on February 15, 2018 for Android and iOS, and on May 22, 2018 for Microsoft Windows via DMM Games. Mobile version would later be released in other regions.
